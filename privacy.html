Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Welcome to the Medical Diagnostic Centre booking application. Your privacy and the security of your personal and medical information are our highest priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Android application and booking portal.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with a seamless booking and diagnostic experience, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Your name, phone number, and any details you provide when booking an appointment.
  • Medical Documents: Files or images you choose to upload (such as previous prescriptions or test requisitions) through the app.
  • Device Information: General information required for the app to function securely and optimize performance.

2. App Permissions

Our Android application requests specific device permissions to function correctly. We only access these features when you actively use them:

  • Camera & Audio: Requested only when you choose to take a photo of a prescription or medical document to upload to our portal.
  • Storage/Media Access: Required to allow you to download lab reports or upload existing documents from your phone's gallery to the booking portal.
  • Notifications: Used to send you timely reminders about your upcoming appointments, lab report availability, and general health checkups.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information strictly for medical and administrative purposes, including:

  • Scheduling, modifying, or canceling your doctor appointments.
  • Redirecting your booking requests to our official WhatsApp Business account for seamless communication.
  • Providing you with downloaded medical reports and test results securely.
  • Improving the functionality, user interface, and overall smooth operation of the app.

4. Data Security & Sharing

We implement strict security measures to protect your data. All medical and personal data entered into the portal is handled confidentially. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data is only shared with our internal medical staff strictly to facilitate your diagnostic tests and doctor consultations.

5. External Links

Our application may contain links to external sites (such as our official social media pages on Facebook, Instagram, or WhatsApp). Please note that we do not control these external platforms, and their own privacy policies govern them.

6. Contact Us
