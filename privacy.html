1. Introduction

Medical Diagnostic Centre (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard your personal information when you use our website and services.

2. Information we collect

We may collect personal information you provide directly via our booking form or WhatsApp messages, including name, phone number, email (if provided), doctor requested, appointment date, and notes. We may also collect technical information such as IP address and browser type for analytics and site security.

3. How we use your information

Your information is used to process appointment requests, contact you about bookings, maintain records, and improve our services. We may store information in Google Sheets and other secure systems used by Medical Diagnostic Centre.

4. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted service providers (e.g., Google Sheets API providers, Twilio for WhatsApp messaging) solely to operate the booking service. These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.

5. Data retention & security

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for bookkeeping, legal compliance, or as required to provide services. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to secure your data, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us at help.medicaldiagnosticcentre@gmail.com with your request.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact
